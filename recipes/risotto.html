Risotto

Description

This is a hearty mushroom risotto recipe.
This recipe avoids the use of wine or cream, making it appealing to more people.
Feeds 3-4.

Ingredients

  • Small Yellow Onion, minced
  • 8-10oz Baby Bella Mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup Arborio Rice
  • 4 cups Unsalted Beef Broth, warmed
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan Cheese, grated
  • 3 tbsp Butter
  • 5 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 3/4 tbsp Dried Thyme
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. If broth is not already warmed, warm in separate dish on the stove.
  2. In a wide pan on low-medium heat, melt butter. Once melted, add onion, garlic, mushrooms, thyme. Add a dash of salt no more than 1/2 tsp. Sweat and cook down until onions are translucent.
  3. Once sweated out, add rice and toast until grains begin to become translucent, about 3 minutes.
  4. Add 1 to 1 1/2 cups broth to the pan. Typically this should be enough broth to just cover the rice. Stir rice slightly and shake pan to loosen grains on the bottom.
  5. Once rice absorbs enough broth to fall below rice line, add another round of broth. At this point taste the broth and begin salting gradually between rounds, to taste.
  6. As the last bit of broth is cooking down, taste the rice. Once rice is al dente and creamy, remove from heat and stir in parmesan cheese. Serve hot.
