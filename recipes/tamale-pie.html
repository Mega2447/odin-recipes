Tamale Pie

Description

A very easy, delicious meal, this Tamale Pie recipe uses simple ingredients to turn a Tex-Mex classic into a dinner for 3-4 with ease.
This recipe has been modified for sensitive palletes, feel free to modify as you please.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 10 oz Red Enchilada Sauce
  • 15 oz Corn, canned, drained
  • 8.5 oz Jiffy Corn Bread Mix
  • 1 tsp Garlic, minced
  • 1/3 cup Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 cup Cheese, cheddar or monterey jack
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin
  • 1/2 tsp Chipotle Powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Brown ground beef, then drain any excess grease. Return to pan.
  3. Stir in garlic, chipotle powder, and cumin. Add enchilada sauce and corn and stir. Let simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  4. If you're not using a cast iron skillet, transfer contents to a pie plate. Top with cheese.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk Jiffy mix with egg and milk. Pour batter over other ingredients. Bake for 20 minutes or until batter is golden brown and no longer wet.
  6. Allow to cool for 10 minutes, then serve hot.
